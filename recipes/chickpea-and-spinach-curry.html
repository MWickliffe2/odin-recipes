Chickpea and Spinach Curry

Description

A healthy and hearty Indian curry from Chetna Makan. This iteration is vegan, but it can also be made with ghee as your cooking oil and then finished at the end with cream.

Ingredients

  • oil of choice
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 brown onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, grated
  • 2 tomatoes, roughly chopped
  • 2 tbsp tomato puree
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 800 grams canned chickpeas
  • 150 grams spinach leaves, roughly chopped
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp double cream (optional)
